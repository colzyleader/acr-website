How do I care for my ACR tee?

To keep your tee looking great:

  • Machine wash cold with like colors
  • Tumble dry low or hang dry
  • Do not bleach
  • Iron inside out on low heat if needed
  • Wash inside out to preserve the chest logo

Following these will help the tee soften with each wash and keep its shape for years.
