Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

We want you to love what you ordered. If something's off, we make it easy.

30-day returns

You have 30 days from the day your order is delivered to return it for a refund or exchange. To qualify, items must be:

  • Unworn and unwashed
  • Free of stains, odors, and pet hair
  • With all original tags attached

How to start a return

Email returns@acrclothing.shop with your order number and the reason for return. We'll send you instructions and a return label within 24 hours.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll refund the original payment method within 5–10 business days. You'll get an email confirmation when the refund is processed. Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Exchanges

Need a different size? Just let us know in your return email. We'll ship the new size as soon as we receive the original — no need to wait for the refund to process.

Damaged or incorrect items

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, email us within 7 days at hello@acrclothing.shop with photos of the issue and your order number. We'll send a replacement or refund — whichever you prefer — and cover return shipping.

Wrong size

Our tee fits true to size. If you ordered the wrong size, no problem — we'll exchange it under the standard return process. Check our size guide if you're unsure before ordering.

Sale and discounted items

Items bought at full price or with a standard discount code are eligible for full returns. Items marked "Final Sale" or sold via flash sale are not eligible for return or exchange.

Lost packages

If your tracking shows delivered but you can't find the package, check with neighbors and your local post office first. If it's still missing after 5 days, email us and we'll work with the carrier on a claim.

Return shipping

Customers cover return shipping unless the return is due to our error (wrong item, defect, damage). We'll provide a prepaid label in those cases.

Contact

Questions? Email hello@acrclothing.shop and we'll get back to you within 1 business day.
