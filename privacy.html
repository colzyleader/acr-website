Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ACR Clothing ("ACR," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit acrclothing.shop or buy a product from us. We keep things simple — we collect only what we need to fulfill orders, send you updates you've asked for, and improve the site.

1. Information we collect

When you visit our site or place an order, we may collect:

  • Order information: name, shipping address, billing address, email, and phone number when you place an order.
  • Payment information: processed securely by our payment provider (Shopify Payments / Stripe). We do not store your card details on our servers.
  • Email signups: if you join our list, we save your email and first name.
  • Site analytics: basic browser, device, and traffic data via cookies and tools like Google Analytics or Vercel Analytics.
  • Communications: any messages you send us via email, contact form, or chat.

2. How we use your information

  • To process and ship your orders.
  • To send order confirmations, shipping updates, and delivery notifications.
  • To send marketing emails about new drops, sales, and brand updates — only if you've subscribed. You can unsubscribe anytime.
  • To answer your support questions.
  • To improve the site and detect fraud.

3. Cookies and tracking

We use cookies for things like keeping items in your cart, remembering your dark/light mode preference, and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the site may stop working.

4. Email marketing

We use Klaviyo to send our welcome series, drop announcements, and occasional offers. Every email has an unsubscribe link. We never sell your email to anyone.

5. Sharing your information

We share your information only with the services we need to run our business:

  • Shopify — to process orders and payments.
  • Klaviyo — to send transactional and marketing emails.
  • Shipping carriers (USPS, UPS, etc.) — to deliver your order.
  • Vercel — our website hosting provider.

We do not sell your personal information.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. To make a request, email us at himc798@gmail.com.

7. Data security

Our checkout uses SSL encryption. Payment information is handled by PCI-compliant providers. We do our best to protect your data, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Minors

Our site is not intended for anyone under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email himc798@gmail.com.
