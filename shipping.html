Shipping Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Here's how we ship and what you can expect after placing an order.

Where we ship

We currently ship across the United States. International shipping is coming soon — join the list to be notified when it goes live.

Shipping rates

  • Standard shipping: $5.99 — 3–5 business days
  • Free standard shipping: on orders over $75
  • Expedited shipping: $14.99 — 2 business days

Processing time

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days. We don't ship on weekends or holidays. You'll receive a shipping confirmation with tracking when your order leaves our warehouse.

Tracking

Every order includes tracking. You'll get an email and (if you opted in) text updates with the tracking number. You can also track your order at any time via the Shop app once you've checked out.

Delivery time

Standard orders typically arrive within 4–7 business days from order placement (1–2 days processing + 3–5 days transit). Expedited orders typically arrive in 3 business days.

Shipping delays

Carrier delays, weather, and high-volume periods (holidays, drops) can occasionally push delivery times. If your order seems stuck, email himc798@gmail.com and we'll look into it.

Lost or stolen packages

If tracking says "delivered" but you can't find your package, check with neighbors and your local post office first. If it's still missing 5 days later, contact us — we'll work with the carrier on a claim and make it right.

Wrong address

Double-check your shipping address before checkout. If your order ships to the wrong address because of a typo, we can't refund or replace it. If you catch the mistake before we ship, email us right away — we'll do our best to update it.

Damaged or missing items

If your order arrives damaged or something's missing, email us within 7 days with photos and your order number. See our Refund Policy for full details.

Contact

Questions about your shipment? Email himc798@gmail.com.
